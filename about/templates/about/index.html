About 1.37:1

My name is Jim Shields, and I've started building this site out of a desire to say something new about film. Though there are many brilliant film writers out there (not to mention those involved in the filmmaking process), I haven't seen much in the way of data analysis for film. I hope I can add to that conversation through interactive visualizations using film data (see "Interactive") and blog posts analyzing the data (see "Blog").
